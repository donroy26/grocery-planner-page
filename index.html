Dinner 1

Sheet Pan Chicken Breasts with Potatoes & Brussels Sprouts 🧒$9.00Fresh Family Pack Chicken Breasts ($2.19, ALDI)
Juicy roasted chicken breasts with crispy baby potatoes and caramelized Brussels sprouts on one pan.
Ingredients
  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1.5 lb baby potatoes
  • 3 cups Brussels sprouts (already on hand)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika

Dinner 2

Cheesy Beef Taco Skillet over Rice 🧒$9.00Fresh Ground Beef Chub ($2.99, ALDI)
One-pan seasoned beef and rice topped with melty cheddar; add hot sauce per plate.
Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1.5 cups dry long-grain white rice
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 15-oz can diced tomatoes or salsa
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1 bottle hot sauce (for adults, per plate)

Dinner 3

Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sandwiches 🧒$8.50Kirkwood Fresh Family Pack Chicken Thighs ($1.55, ALDI)
Tender shredded BBQ chicken piled on soft buns—set it and forget it.
Ingredients
  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 18-oz bottle BBQ sauce
  • 8 pieces hamburger buns
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix (optional topping)

Dinner 4

Honey Garlic Shrimp Stir-Fry with Rice 🧒$13.50Fresh Jumbo Raw Peeled & Deveined Tail-Off Shrimp ($9.49, ALDI)
Sweet-savory honey garlic shrimp over rice with crisp veggies, ready in 20 minutes.
Ingredients
  • 1 lb raw peeled deveined shrimp
  • 1.5 cups dry long-grain white rice
  • 1 16-oz bag frozen stir-fry vegetable blend
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic

Dinner 5

Cheese Tortellini with Marinara & Garden Veggies 🧒$9.00Whole White Mushrooms ($1.29, ALDI)
Pillowy cheese tortellini in marinara with sauteed mushrooms—a quick toddler favorite.
Ingredients
  • 20 oz cheese tortellini
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 8 oz white mushrooms
  • 1 medium zucchini
  • 0.5 cup parmesan cheese
