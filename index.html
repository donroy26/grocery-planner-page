  • 1 container guacamole
  • 1 tsp cumin

Dinner 4

Grilled Lemon-Garlic Salmon with Rice & Green Beans 🧒$16.00Fresh Atlantic Salmon
Flaky lemon-garlic salmon off the grill with fluffy rice and tender green beans.
Ingredients
  • 1.5 lb fresh Atlantic salmon
  • 1.5 cups dry white rice
  • 12 oz green beans
  • 1 piece lemon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
